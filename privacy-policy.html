Hotel Radhika Somnath Privacy Policy

At Hotel Radhika Somnath, we value your trust and are committed to safeguarding your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you browse our website, make a booking, or use our services. By continuing to use our website or services, you agree to the practices outlined in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information from our guests and website visitors:

  • Personal Information: Full name, contact number, email address, postal address, nationality, government-issued identification (Aadhaar, Passport, Driving License), and payment details for reservation and check-in purposes.
  • Booking Information: Dates of stay, room type, special requests, and booking preferences.
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device information, and usage data such as time spent on our site or pages visited.
  • Communication Records: Emails, phone calls, WhatsApp messages, and other interactions with our staff for service or inquiry purposes.
  • Social Media Data: If you connect with us via platforms like Facebook, WhatsApp, or Instagram, we may receive information as permitted by your privacy settings on those platforms.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is collected to provide a seamless guest experience and may be used for:

  • Managing reservations, check-ins, check-outs, and guest services.
  • Communicating booking confirmations, updates, offers, and post-stay feedback requests.
  • Improving website performance, user experience, and hotel services through analytics.
  • Compliance with legal requirements under Indian law, including identity verification.
  • Detecting and preventing fraudulent or unauthorized activities.
  • Sending marketing updates, promotional offers, and event information with your consent.

3. How We Share Your Information

  • Third-Party Service Providers: Data may be shared with secure third-party vendors like payment processors, booking engines, and IT service providers to fulfill reservations and enhance services.
  • Legal Obligations: Information may be disclosed to government authorities or law enforcement when required by law.
  • Business Changes: In case of mergers, acquisitions, or ownership transfers, guest data may be transferred to the new entity, with appropriate safeguards.
  • Marketing Partners: With your consent, limited information may be shared for promotions or loyalty programs.

We never sell or rent your personal information to unrelated third parties for unsolicited marketing.

4. Data Security

We apply industry-standard security measures such as firewalls, encrypted servers, and secure payment gateways to protect your information. While we strive for the highest level of security, no system is 100% risk-free.

5. Your Rights as a Guest

You are entitled to the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request updates or corrections to inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data, except where retention is legally required.
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from promotional emails, WhatsApp messages, or SMS marketing.

Requests can be made by contacting us through the details provided below. Verification of identity may be required for certain requests.

6. Third-Party Websites & Links

Our website may include links to external booking platforms or social media. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external websites. Please review their policies separately before providing any information.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and analytics tools to enhance browsing, monitor traffic, and personalize guest experiences. Cookies can be managed or disabled through your browser settings, though some features of the website may not function properly without them.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our services are intended for guests aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a child’s data has been inadvertently collected, please notify us, and we will take steps to delete it immediately.

9. Policy Updates

Hotel Radhika Somnath reserves the right to update or revise this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect operational, legal, or regulatory changes. The revised policy will be effective once posted on our website. Guests will be notified of significant updates via email or notice on our site.

10. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of India. Any disputes shall fall under the jurisdiction of the courts of Gujarat, India.
