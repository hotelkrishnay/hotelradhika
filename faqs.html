Questions About Somnath Temple

Where is Somnath Temple located?
Somnath Temple is located in Prabhas Patan near Veraval in Saurashtra, Gujarat, on the Arabian Sea coast.
What is the history of Somnath Temple?
Somnath Temple is considered the first of the 12 Jyotirlingas and has been destroyed and rebuilt multiple times due to invasions, with its current structure completed in 1951.
What are the temple timings of Somnath?
The temple remains open daily from 6:00 AM to 9:00 PM. The Aarti takes place three times a day – morning, noon, and evening.
Is there an entry fee to visit Somnath Temple?
No, there is no entry fee to enter the main Somnath Temple. However, there are charges for the light and sound show.
Can we take mobile phones or cameras inside Somnath Temple?
No, mobile phones, cameras, and electronic devices are not allowed inside the temple premises for security reasons.
Is there a light and sound show at Somnath Temple?
Yes, a light and sound show called "Jay Somnath" is held every evening from 8:00 PM, showcasing the temple's history in multiple languages.
What is the best time to visit Somnath Temple?
The best time to visit is between October and March when the weather is pleasant and ideal for darshan and sightseeing.
Is Somnath Temple open on all days?
Yes, the Somnath Temple is open every day of the week, including weekends and public holidays.
Is Somnath Temple a Jyotirlinga?
Yes, Somnath is the first among the 12 sacred Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva in India.
Are there any restrictions for foreigners visiting Somnath Temple?
Foreign tourists are welcome, but must follow dress codes and are not allowed to carry cameras or phones inside the temple.
Is there a dress code for Somnath Temple?
Yes, visitors are expected to wear modest and traditional clothing. Shorts and sleeveless tops are not allowed.
Are there lockers available near Somnath Temple?
Yes, mobile lockers and luggage storage facilities are available near the temple for visitors' convenience.
Can senior citizens get wheelchairs at Somnath Temple?
Yes, wheelchairs are available at the temple premises for elderly and differently-abled devotees.
How much time does it take for darshan in Somnath Temple?
On regular days, darshan takes around 30–45 minutes. During festivals or weekends, it may take longer due to crowds.
Is there a temple complex or other shrines near Somnath?
Yes, the Somnath temple complex includes smaller shrines, a museum, and the Triveni Sangam where three rivers meet.
Can we attend the morning Aarti at Somnath Temple?
Yes, the morning Aarti is held around 7:00 AM and is open to all devotees. It's a peaceful and divine experience.
Are VIP or special darshan passes available at Somnath Temple?
There is no formal VIP pass, but senior citizens and special needs individuals are often given priority in queues.
What is the contact number of Somnath Temple Trust?
You can contact the Shree Somnath Trust at +91-2876-231212 for inquiries related to darshan, bookings, or donations.
Can we donate online to Somnath Temple?
Yes, donations can be made online through the official website of the Shree Somnath Trust using net banking or UPI.
Is prasad available after darshan in Somnath Temple?
Yes, prasad is available for all devotees after darshan and can also be purchased from the temple counter.
