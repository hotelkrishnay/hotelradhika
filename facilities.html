Hotel Radhika - Facilities Available In Hotel

24-Hour Front Desk

Hotel Radhika Somnath provides a reliable 24-hour reception desk with friendly staff ready to assist at any time. Whether you need a smooth late-night check-in, local sightseeing guidance, or urgent travel support, our team ensures a seamless and stress-free stay near Somnath Temple.

Free High-Speed Wi-Fi

Stay connected with our complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi available in guest rooms and public areas. Share your Somnath pilgrimage experiences, stream entertainment, or manage work tasks with our reliable and secure internet connection throughout your stay.

Air-Conditioned Rooms

Relax in air-conditioned rooms designed for comfort after exploring Somnath’s spiritual and cultural landmarks. Every room is equipped with cooling options to provide a refreshing retreat for both families and solo travelers.

Daily Housekeeping

Our daily housekeeping service ensures a clean, hygienic, and well-maintained environment throughout your stay. Rooms are refreshed daily with fresh linens and tidied up so you can enjoy a truly relaxing holiday near Somnath Temple.

Secure Luggage Storage

Travel with ease using our secure luggage storage facility. Guests arriving early or departing late can safely store their belongings while exploring Somnath attractions without any burden.

Elevator Access

Our multi-storey hotel is equipped with a modern elevator for easy access to all floors. This facility is ideal for elderly guests, families with children, and travelers carrying heavy luggage.

Power Backup

Enjoy an uninterrupted stay with our dedicated power backup system. Even during electricity cuts, we ensure continuous lighting, cooling, and essential services for your comfort.

Comfortable Beds

Experience restful sleep on premium-quality beds with clean and cozy linens. Designed to maximize relaxation, our beds make Hotel Radhika a perfect retreat after temple visits and sightseeing tours.

Private Bathrooms

Each room includes a private en-suite bathroom with hot and cold water facilities. Our bathrooms are stocked with basic essentials to provide convenience and a refreshing start to your day.

Towels & Toiletries

Guests enjoy complimentary fresh towels and essential toiletries like soap and shampoo, ensuring a hassle-free and comfortable stay at our budget-friendly hotel near Somnath Temple.

Television

Unwind after your day with in-room television entertainment. Watch local or national channels and stay updated with news while relaxing in your comfortable Somnath accommodation.

Work Desk / Table

All rooms include a compact work desk or table, ideal for guests working remotely, writing, or planning their Somnath sightseeing itinerary.

Wardrobe or Storage Space

Keep your belongings neat with a wardrobe or hanging space. This thoughtful feature ensures your clothes remain organized and your room clutter-free.

Drinking Water

Complimentary safe drinking water is provided in every room, keeping you hydrated and refreshed throughout your pilgrimage or holiday in Somnath.

Parking Facility

We provide secure and convenient parking for cars and small vehicles. Perfect for families and group travelers driving to Somnath.

Airport Pickup Service

Hotel Radhika offers paid airport transfer services from nearby airports including Diu, Keshod, Rajkot, Porbandar, and Jamnagar. Advance booking ensures a smooth, stress-free arrival at our hotel.

Railway Station Pickup Service

We arrange railway station pickups from Veraval, Junagadh, and Somnath stations, providing convenience for guests arriving by train.

Bus Station Pickup Service

Guests arriving by bus can avail our paid bus stand pickup service from Somnath and Veraval bus stations, ensuring a comfortable transfer to Hotel Radhika.

Travel Desk Assistance

Our in-house travel desk helps guests plan their Somnath journey with guided tours, temple visit arrangements, and sightseeing recommendations like Triveni Sangam and Bhalka Tirth.

Wake-Up Call Service

Start your day on time with our wake-up call facility. Perfect for early-morning temple darshan or sunrise views at Somnath Beach.

CCTV Security

Your safety is our top priority. The hotel is monitored with CCTV surveillance in common areas, providing guests with a secure and worry-free environment.

Laundry Service

Guests staying longer can benefit from our affordable laundry service. Clothes are washed and returned promptly, keeping you fresh for temple visits and excursions.

Iron & Ironing Board

On request, we provide iron and ironing board facilities. Ideal for pilgrims and business travelers who want crisp, presentable clothing during their stay.

Conference Room

Hotel Radhika offers an optional conference/meeting room for business travelers, group discussions, or small events, making our hotel versatile for both leisure and work stays.

Newspapers in Lobby

Catch up with the world while relaxing in our lobby with daily newspapers available for guests (subject to availability).

Shared Fridge / Minibar

Guests can use our shared refrigerator or request minibar facilities (in select rooms) to keep snacks and drinks cool during their stay in Somnath.

Dining Options

Although Hotel Radhika Somnath does not have an in-house restaurant, we arrange room delivery from nearby eateries. Guests can enjoy authentic Gujarati cuisine and other meals conveniently within the comfort of their rooms.
